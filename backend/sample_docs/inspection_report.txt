QUARTERLY PLANT COMPLIANCE AND SAFETY INSPECTION REPORT
Plant: IndustrialMind Unit 4
Inspection Reference: INSP-2026-Q1
Inspection Date: 2026-02-28
Auditor: Ramesh Chandra (Lead Safety Auditor)
Regulatory Reference: Indian Factories Act 1948, Section 36 and Section 38

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
An inspection of Unit 4 was carried out to assess compliance with the Factories Act 1948 and internal safety guidelines. Overall compliance rating is Conditional Acceptable. Several safety deviations require immediate corrective action.

DETAILED FINDINGS AND DEVIATIONS

Deviation 1: Lack of proper pressure testing documentation for steam boiler TAG-001.
Location: Boiler House A
Severity: High
Detail: Section 31 of the Factories Act 1948 requires pressure vessels to be certified by a competent person annually. The latest inspection record for TAG-001 was dated January 2025, which exceeds the 12-month limit.
Corrective Action: Schedule third-party hydro-test certification immediately and update records.

Deviation 2: Emergency escape route obstruction.
Location: Near feed pump station housing TAG-002.
Severity: Medium
Detail: Spare metal pipes and structural steel plates were stacked in front of the emergency fire exit door. This violates Section 38 of the Factories Act 1948.
Corrective Action: Relocate metal piping to salvage yard. Fire exit route cleared during audit.

Deviation 3: Confined space gas monitoring logs missing.
Location: Storage Tank TK-502 Area
Severity: High
Detail: Standard operating procedures require daily logs of gas levels before entry. Two entry permit sheets did not have the pre-entry toxic gas measurements recorded.
Corrective Action: Retrain supervisors on Confined Space Entry permit process. Conduct random inspections weekly.

RE-AUDIT SCHEDULE
A follow-up inspection is scheduled for 2026-04-15 to check compliance status and confirm all deviations are closed.

END OF REPORT
